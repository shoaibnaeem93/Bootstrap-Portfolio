About Me

Im interested in sports, music, video games and kicking it with friends. My favorite sport is soccer, which probably accounts for 50% of my life. I play as a central attacking midfielder or a winger (both right and left). My favorite soccer team is Real Madrid. I like and listen to all kinds of music...even a little bit of country. I dont have any preference between different genres so as long as it sounds good and I enjoy it. I cannot exactly label one band or musician as my favorite artist, since my listening mood varies from time to time. Video games have been a huge part of my childhood. The first video game console I remember getting was the Nintendo 64 and I rememeber playing "Super Mario" and "Mario Kart" for hours on end. A few video games titles that I like are: Mario series, Kingdom Hearts, Final Fantasy 7, 8, 10 and 15, Zelda, and no surprise FIFA Soccer series. I really enjoy having a good time with family and friends. Whether it be chilling at someone's house or travelling together, being with friends and friends is always a good time.
